Windows:
	Double click `launch.bat` for a resolution 1/6 of original, or
	Run from command line with `launch.bat x`, where x is the scale you want (ie: 1/x of original)

OS X or Linux:
	To launch from the .sh files, open a terminal and type `./LaunchXXXX.sh`
	You may have to mark the files as executable on your system with `chmod +x Launch*`

All:
	Double click the `Builder-1.2.2.jar` for a resolution 1/8 of original, or
	To launch the jar directly run `java -jar Builder-1.2.2.jar x` where x is an optional integer representing scale, such that the width of the map in the window is 4320/x
